What is AIRCRUISE

Our idea is to reinvent the cruise, that's why we offer you an unforgettable experience, full of sensations and in the idyllic setting of our Airship Balloon.
This concept is simple, to have the head in the cloud for two-weeks, in an all-inclusive trip over Africa. You will fly over the most beautiful landscapes of Africa that you will discover through our stopovers.
40 people are expected for this sensational stay, you will be welcomed on board by 17 crew members. On the First deck of 600 m2 you will find a Restaurant, lounge bar, library, a fitness. On the Second deck of 600 m2too : you'll find 20 cabins for 2 person,
